How It Works

Three steps to sovereign identity

No servers store your data. No company controls your identity. Everything stays on your device and the blockchain.

1

Verify Your Identity

Upload your government ID and take a selfie. Images are hashed locally using SHA-256 — they never leave your device.

2

Biometric Authentication

Scan your fingerprint or face using your phone's built-in security. Your biometric creates a unique cryptographic proof.

3

Blockchain Registration

Your identity hashes are stored on Polygon blockchain. Only you control it. Anyone can verify it. No one can fake it.
